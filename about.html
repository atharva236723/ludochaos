The story behind Ludo Chaos

Ludo Chaos is a fast, friendly take on the board game you grew up with — rebuilt for the browser with zero installs and zero waiting. Gather friends on one device, or take on the computer, and roll your way home.

Two ways to play

  • Classic — the traditional rules, faithfully recreated.
  • Chaos — capture before entering your home stretch, a bonus turn after every capture (but the striking pin can't move on it), and a double 6 reverses your direction for 4 moves.

Built simply, on purpose

The whole game is hand-crafted vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript — no frameworks, no trackers, no fuss. Just open it and play.
