About the game

What is Ludo Chaos?

Ludo Chaos is a free Ludo online game you can play instantly in your browser — no downloads, no accounts, no waiting. Ludo itself is a classic strategy board game for two to four players, descended from the centuries-old Indian game Pachisi: each player races four tokens out of their base, clockwise around a cross-shaped track, and into the central home, with every move decided by the roll of a die. It is famously easy to learn yet rich with tactics — which is exactly why Ludo Chaos gives you two ways to play.

Classic Ludo

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ludo and Ludo Chaos.

What is Ludo?

Ludo is a classic strategy board game for 2–4 players descended from the ancient Indian game Pachisi. Each player races four tokens out of their colored base, clockwise around a cross-shaped track, and into the central home. Every move is decided by a single die roll, making it easy to learn yet full of tactical choices about which token to move.

How to play Ludo?

Roll the die on your turn. You need a 6 to bring a token out of base onto the track. From there, move any active token the number of squares shown. Land on a lone opponent to capture it and send it back to base — earning a bonus turn. Safe squares (marked with a star or your own color) protect tokens from capture. Get all four tokens home first to win. Rolling a 6, capturing an opponent, or finishing a token earns an extra roll.

What are the rules of Ludo online?

Ludo Chaos offers two online rule sets. Classic Ludo follows traditional rules: roll a 6 to leave base, move tokens clockwise around the 52-square track, land on a lone opponent to capture, rest on star and colored safe squares where no one can touch you, and reach home with an exact roll — overshooting is never allowed.

Chaos Ludo adds a capture-first gate (a token may not enter its home stretch until you've sent at least one opponent back) and direction-reversing double sixes — roll double 6 to reverse your direction for 4 moves.

What is the 3 six rule in Ludo?

In some traditional Ludo variants, rolling three sixes in a row forfeits the third six and ends your turn immediately. Ludo Chaos does enforce this rule in Classic mode — rolling three sixes in a row forfeits your turn. In Chaos mode there is no such cap, so every six earns a bonus roll.

How many 6s are allowed in Ludo?

In traditional Ludo, house rules often cap consecutive sixes at two or three. In Ludo Chaos, Classic mode caps at three — the third six in a row forfeits your turn. In Chaos mode there is no cap — each six earns you another roll. Rolling a six also lets you choose between moving a token out of base or advancing an active one, giving you extra strategic options.

Can two people play Ludo online?

Yes! Ludo Chaos fully supports two-player games. In a two-player match each person controls two colors. You can play pass-and-play on a single screen with a friend, or face a computer opponent solo. Four-player games are also supported, and you can mix human and computer players in any combination.

What is the history behind Ludo?

Ludo is rooted in Pachisi, a game played in India as far back as the 6th century AD and depicted in the caves of Ajanta. British colonizers encountered Pachisi during the 19th century and simplified it — replacing the cloth board and cowrie-shell dice with a compact cross-shaped board and a standard cubic die. The game was patented in England in 1896 under the name "Ludo" (Latin for I play) and quickly became a family staple. Today, Ludo is enjoyed worldwide in physical and digital forms.

What are the rules for killing in Ludo?

A kill (capture) happens when you land your token on the exact square occupied by a single opponent token — that token is sent back to its base and must roll a 6 again to re-enter. You earn a bonus turn for each capture. Captures are blocked on safe squares (the starred central square and colored entry squares). In Chaos mode, making at least one capture is required before any token may enter the home stretch — so attacking opponents is not just rewarded, it's necessary.
