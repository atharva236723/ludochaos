Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters. The short version: Ludo Chaos runs entirely in your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party trackers. Game state lives only in the current browser tab and is gone when you close it.

Cookies & storage

We do not set advertising or tracking cookies. Any preferences (such as the sound toggle) stay on your own device and are never sent anywhere.

Questions

Anything you'd like to ask about privacy? Reach us through the Contact page — we're happy to help.
